About Me

I am a lawyer, senior executive and full stack web developer. I have a passion for simplifying, synthesizing and disseminating information. I want to teach every child to code and help every senior to easily use their new mobile devices. I love to teach and I firmly believe that every concept can be simplified so it can be explained to a five year old - whether this is why the sky is blue or how to code IO apps with Swift.

In addition to teaching, I've discovered that there are a lack of apps aimed at Seniors that help them ease the transition to working on a mobile device for perhaps the first time. This transition should be able to be done with enthusiasm and not frustration.

And finally, I want to build games. I first learned DOS because I needed to install drivers to get my games to work. Over the years my children have been the happy recipients of my gaming passion. I always had the latest platform. While Mario Kart will always be a favourite, I'm somehow addicted to time management games on my I. I am also compulsively interested in learning the psychology behind games; What is it that addicts us and keeps us playing all night?
